Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College

Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College (AKGEC), established in 1998 in Ghaziabad, is affiliated with AKTU and approved by AICTE. It offers NBA-accredited B.Tech, M.Tech, and MCA programs. The vibrant 40-acre Wi-Fi campus boasts modern labs, strong placements, and a robust library spreading over 100,000+ resources.
